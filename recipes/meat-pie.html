Meat-pie

This Ghanaian delicacy is good as main meal together with beverage or as dessert

    Ingredients

    Meat Pie Filling
  • 1 tablespoon sunflower oil
  • 300g minced beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon all purpose seasoning
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 small maggi stock cube
  • 1 large green pepper, chopped into small cubes
    • Pastry
  • 400g self raising flour
  • 255g cold margarine or butter
  • pinch of salt
  • 60ml cold water
  • 1 egg, whisked

    Steps

    Minced Beef Filling
  1. Add a little oil to a large frying pan and heat up.Add the mince and onions and cook on medium heat until if browns
  2. Add in tomato puree,cook for 2 minutes and add cube and salt. Remove form heat and leave to cool , then stir in chopped green peppers.
    3. Pastry
  3. Sift flour and salt into a large mixing bowl
  4. Add margarine to the flour and rub in using your fingertips. Add all the cold water at once and use your fingers bring the pastry together
  5. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured work surface and knead very lightly
  6. Shape the dough into a disk and wrap tightly in plastic wrap and regfrigerate until well chilled, at least an hour or up to overnight
    7. To assemble
  7. Pre-heat oven to 180oC
  8. Line a laarge baking tray with greaseproof paper.
  9. Roll out chilled dough using a rolling pin. Turn pastry as you roll.
  10. cut doung into circles using saucer or a large circle cutter. Place a cutter cup of filling in the centre of the circle
  11. Fold doung over making it into a semicircle. Moisten edges and pinch sides together with a fork. Use a fork and poke holes on the top of the meat pies
  12. Place meat pies ont he baking tray. Brush the tops of pies with egg wash ad bake iv oven for 25 minutes or until the pies are golden brown
